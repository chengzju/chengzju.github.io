About Me

Hello, my name is Cheng Peng and I am currently pursuing a Doctor's degree in Zhejiang University, fortunately advised by Prof. Gang Chen in Hangzhou High-Tech Zone (Binjiang) Institute of Blockchain and Data Security. My research interests include deep learning and data mining in multi-modal learning, few-shot learning, multi-label learning and LLM technologies. Additionally, I am actively exploring multi-modal learning related to large language models.
